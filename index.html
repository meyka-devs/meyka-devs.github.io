What is Meyka?

Meyka is an AI-powered stock market research platform built by Meyka AI PTY LTD in Melbourne, Australia. It combines real-time market data with machine learning to help investors research stocks, screen opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

The platform covers stocks, ETFs, crypto, and commodities across major global exchanges. It is free to use for individual investors, with a developer API available at api.meyka.com for building custom stock market chatbots and applications.

Unlike general-purpose AI tools like ChatGPT, Meyka has real-time stock data built in. It does not search the internet for prices. It knows them. That means faster, more accurate answers for stock research queries.

How the AI Stock Grade Works

Every stock on Meyka gets a score out of 100 and a letter grade from A+ to F. The grade is calculated using 8 weighted factors:

S&P 500 Benchmark (11%)

How the stock performs relative to the S&P 500 index.

Sector Comparison (16%)

Performance measured against sector peers.

Industry Comparison (16%)

How it ranks within its specific industry group.

Financial Growth (12%)

Revenue and earnings growth trajectory.

Key Metrics (16%)

P/E ratio, ROE, EPS, market cap, and valuation metrics.

AI Forecasts (8%)

Machine learning price prediction alignment.

Analyst Consensus (14%)

Wall Street analyst ratings and price targets.

Fundamental Growth (7%)

Long-term fundamental health indicators.

The grade does not give buy or sell recommendations. It is a research tool that helps investors quickly assess stock quality relative to the broader market.
